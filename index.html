Our Story

About Zuru Computers Enterprise

We are a Kenyan tech company dedicated to empowering people with digital skills and reliable technology services.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to make technology simple and affordable for every Kenyan. We train people with practical computer skills that help them find jobs, start businesses, and improve their daily lives.

👁️ Our Vision

To be Kenya's most trusted and recognized computer training and tech services center — known for quality, affordability, and producing real results for our students and clients.

💎 Our Values

Honesty, quality, affordability, and genuine care for every student and customer. We treat everyone who walks through our door with respect and patience.

🤝 Our Promise

We promise to give you the best training and services at a fair price. If you are not happy, we will make it right. Your success is our success.

Who We Are

A Team That Truly Cares

Zuru Computers Enterprise was started with one simple goal: to help Kenyans get the tech skills and services they need to succeed in today's digital world. We started small, but our passion for teaching and helping people has helped us grow into a trusted name in our community.

Whether you are a student who wants to learn Microsoft Office, a business owner who needs printing services, or someone whose laptop is not working — we are here for you. No problem is too small or too big for our team.

We speak your language, understand your needs, and work hard every day to deliver results that make a real difference in your life.
